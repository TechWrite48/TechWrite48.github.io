Who am I?

Howdy! I'm Nicolas Soto, a Technical Communication student at the University of North Texas, aiming to become a technical writer. I specialize in creating documentation that is both accurate and readable. The primary tenet of my writing is to close the gap of ambiguity between written words and their intended meaning.

A key area of my focus is the evolving role of new technologies, particularly how generative AI can be ethically and effectively utilized to enhance professional communication.

To see my skills in action, you can "fish up" my past projects with the interactive game to the left, or find them listed directly below.
